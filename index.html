Job profile Creator


The aim of this project is to develops an online search Portal for the Placement Dept. of the college. The system is an online application that can be accessed throughout the organization and outside as well with proper login provided. T system can be used as an Online Job Portal for the Placement Dept of the college to manage the student information with regards to placement. Students logging should be able to upload their information in the form of a CV. Visitors/Company representatives logging in can search information put up by Students which directly links to their Profile, Wherever they want. It reduces the burden of Resume building for students and helps Placement easily find out the suitable candidates.
